About

Welcome to UQ Course Experiences, a site where students can share their experiences and feedback of UQ courses so new students can see which courses are worth taking.

The goal of this site is to hopefully inform students whether the courses they are considering are interesting, educational or just not worth it (CSSE2002).

Please not that this is an unofficial site which is not affiliated with UQ in any way.
